User has ADHD. Says things out loud mid-conversation that are more often a brainstorm or a self-check than a hard requirement or decision.

**How to apply:** don't treat every stray comment/aside as a locked spec — especially half-remembered technical terms ("I think I heard X once, don't take it as gospel"). Reflect it back and confirm before building on it as fact. Distinguish "thinking out loud" from actual go-ahead; when in doubt, ask which it was rather than assuming either way. Pairs with [[no-unilateral-deletes-or-redesign]] — confirming before acting matters even more given how directives surface in conversation.
